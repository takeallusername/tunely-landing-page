Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2025

1. Personal Information We Collect

Tunely collects the following personal information:

  • Account information: Email address, name
  • Payment information: Payment method, billing address (processed through payment providers)
  • Usage information: Created projects, credit usage, service usage history
  • Technical information: IP address, browser type, access time

2. Purpose of Collection and Use

  • Service provision and operation
  • Member management and identity verification
  • Payment processing and billing
  • Customer support and inquiry handling
  • Service improvement and statistical analysis
  • Marketing and promotions (with consent)

3. Retention and Use Period

Personal information is retained during the service usage period and destroyed without delay upon membership withdrawal. However, it will be retained for the required period if legally mandated:

  • Records regarding contracts or subscription withdrawal: 5 years
  • Records regarding payment and supply of goods: 5 years
  • Records regarding consumer complaints or dispute resolution: 3 years

4. Disclosure to Third Parties

Tunely does not disclose user personal information to third parties in principle. Exceptions may include:

  • User has given prior consent
  • Disclosure is mandatory under law

4.1 Service Partnerships

We integrate with the following services to provide functionality:

  • Vercel: Application deployment
  • Supabase: Database services
  • Payment processors: Payment processing

5. Processing Entrustment

We entrust personal information processing as follows for better service provision:

  • AWS/Google Cloud: Server hosting and data storage
  • Email service providers: Notification and marketing email delivery

6. Destruction of Personal Information

Personal information is destroyed without delay when the collection and use purpose is achieved or the retention period expires. Electronic files are deleted in an unrecoverable manner, and paper documents are shredded or incinerated.

7. User Rights

Users may exercise the following rights at any time:

  • Request to view personal information
  • Request to correct or delete personal information
  • Request to suspend personal information processing
  • Account deletion (membership withdrawal)

Rights can be exercised directly in the settings page or by contacting customer support.

8. Technical Measures for Privacy Protection

  • Encrypted password storage
  • Secure data transmission via HTTPS
  • Access control management and logging
  • Regular security checks and updates

9. Cookie Usage

Tunely uses cookies to provide services. Cookies are used to maintain login status, save settings, etc., and can be disabled through browser settings.

10. Privacy Officer

For questions regarding personal information processing, please contact:

Privacy Officer
Email: privacy@tunely.app

11. Privacy Policy Changes

This privacy policy may be revised according to changes in laws and services. Changes will be announced at least 7 days in advance, and significant changes 30 days in advance.
